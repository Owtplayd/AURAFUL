AVRA

A text-command-based Aura collecting RPG

Basic Commands:

  • /help - Show available commands
  • /daily - Claim daily Aura bonus
  • /inventory - Check your items
  • /shop - Buy items
  • /quest - Start an adventure
  • /minigame - Play mini-games

Command Combos:

Discover powerful command combinations to earn bonus Aura!
